Head Sizing Tool

This application measures your Head Width & Height using your webcam to categorize your perfect fit.

Calibration

Select your biological sex to estimate Eye Distance (IPD) for scale.

Part 1: Head Width

1. Use the Bubble Level to balance your head.
2. Place your Index Fingers on both Tragus (ear bumps).
3. Look straight at the camera (not screen).

Part 2: Head Height

1. Use the Bubble Level to balance your head.
2. Place one of your Index fingers on either tragus (ear bumps).
3. Press your Flat Palm firmly on the Top of your Head with the other hand.
4. Look straight at the camera (not screen).
